### Dedicated Section Pages

Each section has its own dedicated page with multilingual metadata, SEO optimization, and Schema.org structured data:

#### 1. Quran Section (`/sections/quran` or `/{lang}/sections/quran`)
- **Content**: Complete Quran text with all 114 Surahs
- **Features**: 
  - Arabic text with proper diacritics
  - Multiple translations in all supported languages
  - Verse-by-verse reading
  - Surah navigation
  - Bookmark functionality
  - Share and download verses as PNG/PDF
- **Keywords**: Quran, Holy Quran, Quran recitation, Quran verses, Quran surahs, Quran reading, Islamic charity, Meshari Alabra
- **API**: Al Quran Cloud API with 30-day caching

#### 2. Tafseer Section (`/sections/tafseer` or `/{lang}/sections/tafseer`)
- **Content**: Quranic interpretation and exegesis
- **Features**:
  - Multiple tafseer sources (Ibn Kathir, Tabari, Al-Muyassar)
  - Search by Surah and Ayah number
  - Multilingual interpretations
  - Clean HTML rendering
- **Keywords**: Tafseer, Quran interpretation, Ibn Kathir, Tabari, Quran exegesis, Quran commentary, Quran meaning

#### 3. Dhikr Section (`/sections/dhikr` or `/{lang}/sections/dhikr`)
- **Content**: Digital tasbih/dhikr counter
- **Features**:
  - Three dhikr options: SubhanAllah, Alhamdulillah, Allahu Akbar
  - Milestone tracking (33, 99, 100)
  - Reset functionality
  - Haptic feedback on mobile
- **Keywords**: Dhikr, remembrance of Allah, tasbih, tahlil, takbir, daily dhikr, Islamic remembrance

#### 4. Prayer Times Section (`/sections/prayer-times` or `/{lang}/sections/prayer-times`)
- **Content**: Accurate prayer times based on location
- **Features**:
  - Automatic geolocation detection
  - Manual location selection
  - All 5 daily prayers (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha) + Sunrise
  - Hijri calendar date
  - Gregorian date
  - Location display with multilingual support
  - Prayer time notifications (optional)
- **Keywords**: Prayer times, salah times, Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha, Islamic prayer, prayer schedule
- **API**: Aladhan API with 6-hour caching

#### 5. Qibla Section (`/sections/qibla` or `/{lang}/sections/qibla`)
- **Content**: Qibla direction finder with compass
- **Features**:
  - Real-time compass direction to Kaaba
  - Distance to Makkah calculation
  - Device orientation support (iOS and Android)
  - Geolocation-based direction
  - Beautiful compass UI with cardinal directions
- **Keywords**: Qibla direction, Qibla compass, Mecca, Kaaba, Qibla finder, direction to Kaaba

#### 6. Donation Section (`/sections/donation` or `/{lang}/sections/donation`)
- **Content**: Orphan sponsorship and charity information
- **Features**:
  - Direct integration with Ehsan.sa campaign
  - Benefits of orphan sponsorship explained
  - Islamic principles of charity
  - Donation call-to-action
- **Keywords**: Orphan sponsorship, ongoing charity, donation, charity, sadaqah, zakat, orphan care, Islamic charity
- **Campaign URL**: https://ehsan.sa/campaign/6FC11E15DA

#### 7. Supplications Section (`/sections/supplications` or `/{lang}/sections/supplications`)
- **Content**: Daily supplications and duas
- **Features**:
  - Supplications for the deceased
  - Morning and evening adhkar
  - Categorized supplications (guidance, forgiveness, gratitude, health, etc.)
  - Multilingual translations
- **Keywords**: Supplications, dua, prayers for deceased, daily supplications, Islamic prayers, duas

#### 8. Hadith Section (`/sections/hadith` or `/{lang}/sections/hadith`)
- **Content**: Prophetic traditions and sayings
- **Features**:
  - Random hadith generator
  - Multiple categories (charity, speech, religion, brotherhood, knowledge, mercy, faith, help, love, etc.)

#### 9. YouTube Section (`/sections/youtube` or `/{lang}/sections/youtube`)
