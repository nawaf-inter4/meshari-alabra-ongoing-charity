## Site Purpose
This website is a memorial and ongoing charity platform dedicated to Meshari Ahmed Sulaiman Alabra, who passed away on March 29, 2023, in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. The site serves as a continuous source of charitable deeds (Sadaqah Jariyah) in Islamic tradition.

## Core Features
1. Quran Recitations & Reading
- Interactive Quran reader
- Audio recitations
- Multiple translations
- Tafseer (interpretations)

2. Prayer Tools
- Prayer times calculator
- Qibla direction finder
- Dhikr counter
- Digital tasbeeh

3. Charitable Activities
- Orphan sponsorship
- Continuous charity programs
- Islamic educational resources
- Donation management

4. Memorial Features
- Biographical information
- Supplications for the deceased
- Continuous dua (prayers)
- Memorial wall
